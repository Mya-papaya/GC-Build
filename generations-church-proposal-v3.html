Pricing

Two Ways to Build It

I’ve laid out two genuinely different approaches to building your site. Rather than just comparing prices, this section explains the long-term impact of each choice—including what it costs and which path I believe fits your mission best.

What's the actual difference?
Both options give you a site like the one shown above. The difference is how it's built, where it lives, and what that means long-term.
⚙️ Custom Build — Hand-coded
I write the HTML, CSS, and JavaScript from scratch. The site lives on Netlify (free hosting). A separate tool called Contentful acts as the admin dashboard — your team logs in there to update events, slides, and content. Nothing is tied to a platform. It's fully yours.
🟦 Squarespace — Template platform
I custom-configure your site using the Squarespace framework. While I handle the professional design and layout, Squarespace manages the technical side—hosting, security, and updates. Once I’m done, your team can easily make changes through a streamlined visual editor. It’s a faster build, though it does require a $16/mo subscription to Squarespace to keep the site live and secure.
⭐ Recommended
Option A · Custom Build
Hand-coded site + Contentful CMS
Built from scratch using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Hosted free on Netlify. Your team updates everything through a simple Contentful dashboard — no code, no developer needed for day-to-day content.
